Q.10 Why should background and color be used as separate properties?

Separating the background and color properties in CSS allows for better control and specificity when styling elements.

Easy of Maintain:-
When making changes, having separate properties for background and text color simplifies updates. You can modify background visuals without affecting text colors, or vice versa, ensuring consistency and ease of maintenance across a website.
Browser Support:-
Sometimes, background images or complex backgrounds might not load due to various reasons (slow connections, browser issues, etc.). Having a separate color property ensures that even if the background doesn't load, there's a fallback color to maintain readability and aesthetics.
